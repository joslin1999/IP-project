Day 01 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Pick from airport at 10:00 by coach. Transfer to a Culinary Institute 10:30-11:00 Tour of the facility 11:00-11:45 Introduction to the History of the Institution 11:00-11:45 Hands’on Workshop on a Culinary Topic 11:45-12:30 Certifications 1:00 Snacks & Bid Adieu! Early evening over attractions on foot starting from Sun tech city, fountain of wealth, Marina Bay, Gardens by the Bay, Helix bridge, Marina overpass, Merlion Park *Restricted entry at few attractions Pick from Merlion Park & drop at Indian Restaurant for dinner. Post dinner, drop at Hotel

DAY 02 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post early bf visit one of the top ranked schools in the world for the campus tour. Post visit, proceed towards Sentosa Islands. Attractions included are Images of Singapore , Madame Tussauds & Wings of Time. Transfer to Indian restaurant for dinner. Back to hotel at night.

Day 03 : Hotel Management Study Tour

After breakfast, Post breakfast check out & proceed towards Universal Studios. Spend an idyllic day at Southeast Asia’s first and only Universal Studios theme park, featuring 24 rides, shows and attractions in seven themed zones. Evening leave for Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Transit time: 4 hours. Drop at Hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 04 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Transfer to Indian Restuarant for early lunch. Post lunch visit sightseeing attractions such as Petronas Tower, KLCC, Menara Kuala Lumpur & Masjid India. Transfer to Indian Restaurant for dinner. Drop at Hotel for an overnight stay

Day 05 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post B/F, pick up visit to Chocolate Factory tour. Post visit lunch at Indian Restaurant. Later visit a premier culinary school for a 2-hour hands-on training session on Bakery products. Get Individual student Certification for the crash course. In the evening visit National Mosque, King’s Palace & Independence Square for shopping. Drop at Indian Restaurant for dinner. Back to hotel for an overnight stay

Day 06 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post B/F check out & transfer to Genting highlands. Genting Highlands is acknowledged as Malaysia’s premier hillside station; it is Kuala Lumpur’s mountain peak getaway & also known as Asian-style Las Vegas. Renowned for its casino, nightlife and luxury hotels, it features the First World Hotel – which was listed as the world’s largest hotel in 2006 by the Guinness World Book of Records. Stay at First World hotel. A session by one of the senior manager at First World & guided tour of the complete property. Overnight stay.

Day 07 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Check out from hotel & proceed towards Kuala Lumpur. Enroute visit Strawberry Farm for a quick session. On our way visit sighseeing attractions Batu Caves etc. Back to hotel for an overnight stay. Meals at Indian Resturant.

Day 08 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post B/F check out & proceed towards one of worlds largest hospitality schools 9.30am-10.00am: Campus Tour 10.00am – 10.30am: Introduction & history by General Manager 10.30am-11.00am: Q & A Session 11.00am – 12.00pm: Chef Demonstration plus Food Tasting 12.00pm – 12.30pm: Collect Individual Certificate of Participation 12.30pm: Lunch & Depart from the institution Post-session proceed towards Kuala Lumpur International Airport to take a flight back to hometown.
