Russia

Department : Hotel Management 9 Days

Russia, the largest country on our planet Earth.
Russia is country of multiple cultures and languages
quite like India. It has the most comprehensive programs
of offerings for the students. Spanning the entire
breadth of tourist interests starting Art, Palaces, Kremlins,
Cultural programs and Military tours, to name just a few.

Get in Touch with us:










Day 01 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Board flight and arrive at Airport, Moscow Transfer to Hotel. Post lunch at Indian Restaurant start for a city tour. Visit the famous Red Square, the nerve center of Russia since the time of Tsars. Later visit the seat of power – Kremlin and the nearby St Basil’s Cathedral. Transfer to hotel and stay at hotel.

DAY 02 : Hotel Management Study Tour

After early breakfast, start with culinary institute for an interactive session by Russian Culinary Experts. In the evening dress in your best gowns & tuxedos for an evening at Bolshoi theatre enjoying the plays, acts, Opera, Ballets dances, etc. Stay at the hotel.

Day 03 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post breakfast visit Culinary School for interaction / Demonstration with renowned Chefs of international repute. Later visit International Bartender Institute, a visit to learn the craft of bartending and experience of professionals from throughout Russia. A live demonstration and Q & A session by experts. Post lunch at Indian restaurant visit the historic 15th century Arbat street known for its Cafes, Shopping areas and local souvenir shops. Transfer to hotel and stay at hotel.

Day 04 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post early lunch start for Distillery Tour. Later visit Gorky Park overlooking the Moskva river. A Moscow Metro tour in the evening to experience the world’s most swanky and efficient metro system with a palace like interiors, Soviet-era chandeliers at most underground stations. Post dinner at restaurant transfer to Leningradsky Railway station. Board overnight train to St Petersburg

Day 05 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Arrive in Saint Petersburg, popularly known as ‘Venice of North’. Transfer to hotel. Later visit Hotel Management Institute for idea exchange on various catering, hospitality and tourism business. In the evening visit State Hermitage Museum enclosing the gigantic winter palace of Peter the great, old & new Hermitage palace. Later free time for shopping & leisure at Nevsky Prospekt, the city’s famous evening get together street.

Day 06 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post early lunch start for a city tour in St Petersburg famous City Cruises. Famous attractions to be covered are as follows  St.Michael’s Castle  Peter and Paul Fortress  The Bronze Horseman  Admiralty  Mariinsky Theatre  Drawbridge of St Petersburg  Canals tour Evening free for leisure & shopping

Day 07 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post breakfast whole day at leisure.

Day 08 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Post breakfast at hotel visit Divo Ostrov or similar, an amusement park in the city. Enjoy thrill& adventure rides. Evening spend at English Embankment street known as a shopping destination and the Potsuluev Bridge also known as ‘Kissing Bridge’ folklore of romance and happy memories in the city of St Petersburg Transfer to the railway station to board the train to Moscow. Overnight to Moscow

Day 09 : Hotel Management Study Tour

Reach Moscow and proceed to Moscow Airport Board flight
