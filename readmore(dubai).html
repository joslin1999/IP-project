Dubai

Department : Architecture 5 Days

Dubai’s rapid transformation over the past few years means that
it is a fascinating destination for architecture students.
Pride of place is the Burj Khalifa, a feat of engineering.
Equally impressive are other skyscrapers such as the
Burj Al Arab and the Al Fattan Tower. The Dubai Marinais the world’s
largest man-made marina and features a further six towers.

Day 01 : Architecture Study Tour

Assemble at airport and board flight to Dubai. Arrive at Dubai International Airport. Transfer to hotel. Post lunch visit The Opus – The Opus’s approach the design, development, and management of each Omniyat property as if it were a unique work of art. In evening visit Dubai’s famous Dhow Cruise loaded with entertainment, traditional dance, female vocalist and a seven-course dinner meal. Overnight stay at hotel.

DAY 02 : Architecture Study Tour

Early morning visit Burj – Al Arab: One of the world’s most famous and luxurious 5 star hotel. The breakfast will be at the ultra luxurious restaurant. A complete 3 course breakfast followed by a walk through of this marvellous hotel. Later visit Palm Jumeirah an artificial archipelago in the United Arab Emirates, created using land reclamation by Nakheel which extends into the Persian Gulf. Post Lunch starts for Dubai City Tour. Visit Dubai Museum, Jumeirah Mosque, Gold Souk and Atlantis – The Palm, Dubai Frame. Evening free for students to explore Dubai Mall and just beside it experience the tallest structure on earth – Burj Khalifa. Dinner at an Indian restaurant. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 03 : Architecture Study Tour

Post breakfast start for an exciting excursion to Abu Dhabi.Covering Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Emirate palace & visit other signifigant architecture structures of Abu Dhabi. An optional tour to Ferrari World & Yas Islands. Evening back to hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 04 : Architecture Study Tour

Post Lunch start for an exciting evening Desert Safari Desert Safari Includes: Drive through the desert along Al Awir Road – Dune Bashing. – 20-30 minutes Dune Driving – Sun Set Photography – Camel Rides – Fire show and Tanura show – Henna Painting – Arabic Coffee (gahwa) and Fresh Dates – Traditional costumes – Unlimited soft drinks, tea coffee, and mineral water – Buffet Dinner and B.B.Q. (vegetarian food also available on request) Transfer to hotel and overnight stay.

Day 05 : Architecture Study Tour

Post breakfast visit Cayan Tower world’s tallest high – rise building with a twist of 90 degrees. Post lunch proceed to airport to board the flight to hometown with happy memories and a great learning experience.
